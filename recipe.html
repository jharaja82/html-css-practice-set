Pancake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Gradually add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients, stirring until combined.
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and lightly grease it.
  5. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip.
  6. Do not press the pancakes with a spatula, as it can make them dense!
  7. Serve warm with syrup or toppings of choice.

*For fluffier pancakes, let the batter rest for 5 minutes before cooking.*
